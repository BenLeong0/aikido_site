Location

The Koshinkan Aikido Society is based in Sutton just off the high street. The club is easily accessible via public transport from Wimbledon, Merton, Mitcham, Croydon, Cheam, Carshalton, Clapham, and Morden.

Sutton Rail Station is in walking distance. The following buses are local to: 80, 151, 154, 164, 213, 280, 407, 408, 413, 420, 421, 440, 598, 726, S1, S3, S4.

We also have onsite parking for those who wish to drive to the club.
