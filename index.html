Welcome to the Koshinkan Aikido website

We are a small, friendly organisation based in Sutton, Surrey and affiliated to the U.K. Shinwakai and members of the British Aikido Board. The club is led by Sensei John Rodgers. Our Aikido may be described as Traditional with Yoshinkan influence.
