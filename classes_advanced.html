Intermediate / Advanced Level

For students at the intermediate and advanced level, we develop the fundamental techniques taught to beginners plus additional forms and variations. These variations include Tachi Dori (Sword taking), Jo dori (Staff taking), Tanto dori (Knife taking).

For your own safety it is advisable that you discuss your level of ability with Sensei John Rodgers before attending the lesson.
