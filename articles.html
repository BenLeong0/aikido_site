The Koshinkan Aikido Society produces a quarterly newsletter in which articles written by the clubs members are published as well as any news about seminars, upcoming gradings etc. Everyone is encouraged to contribute their views, ideas, reviews and so on with the aim of encouraging thought and debate about Aiki, religion, other martial arts etc.
